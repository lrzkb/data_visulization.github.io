Earnings changes over time

Click the selection box to see how esports players' earnings have changed in a certain game during last decade.

We explore how esports revenue has changed for various games rom 2010 to 2020, We can observe from the line graph that Dota2 and Fortnite have the highest total player earnings. This could be due to the massive number of players in these two games, as well as the big prize money. Furthermore, we can see that, in 2020, players total income has decreased, presumably because to the new crown epidemic.
